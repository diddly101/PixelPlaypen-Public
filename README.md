## 📱 About PixelPlaypens

PixelPlaypens is an iOS app that provides creative tools for editing videos and photos:

- **Video to GIF** - Convert video clips into animated GIFs
- **Video Watermarking** - Add custom watermarks to videos
- **Photo Watermarking** - Watermark single or multiple photos
- **Combine Videos** - Merge multiple video clips
- **Video Teaser** - Create engaging preview clips

## 🔒 Privacy First

PixelPlaypens is built with privacy as a top priority:

- ✅ No data collection
- ✅ No analytics or tracking
- ✅ No third-party services
- ✅ Works completely offline
- ✅ All processing happens on your device
